Dress Like BINI Mikha with Abo: Minimalist and Streetwear Styles Inspired by Your Favorite Artist

By: Shamel Blanquero

If you’re inspired by how pop star BINI Mikha effortlessly pulls off streetwear style in the morning and minimalist fashion at night, this blog is for you.

Discover how to dress like a star as we decode the secrets behind minimalist elegance and streetwear coolness. Whether you’re drawn to sleek simplicity or urban edge, we’ll guide you through creating looks that capture your favorite artist’s essence with Abo.

What is Minimalist-Style Clothing?

Minimalist clothing emphasizes simplicity, functionality, and elegance. This style focuses on fine-tuning your personal look with pieces that match your body shape and skin tone.

How to Dress Minimalist

  • Start with the Basics: Begin with an Abo White Crew Neck T-shirt as your base. You can keep it casual with a pair of jeans.

    • Abo White Crew Neck T-shirt (Php.499)
  • Layering: Add a jacket or blazer for extra style. Pair it with white sneakers or loafers to maintain the minimalist aesthetic.

    • Abo Gray Blazer (Php.999)
  • Accessorize: Finish your look with sunglasses and simple jewelry for added balance.
  • Get Inspired: Check BINI Mikha's Instagram posts for outfit ideas and visualize how to incorporate minimalist fashion into your own style.

    • Abo Gray Blazer (Php.999)

What is Streetwear-Style Clothing?

Streetwear blends casual, urban-inspired clothing with high-fashion elements, emphasizing comfort and a cool, relaxed look.

How to Dress Streetwear

  • Choose Your Tops: Opt for oversized or graphic tees and hoodies. Layer with bomber jackets, denim jackets, or parkas. Pair with baggy jeans, joggers, or cargo pants.

    • Abo Varsity Jacket (Php.899)
  • Select Footwear: Wear iconic or chunky sneakers to achieve a hip-hop or skater-inspired look.
  • Accessorize: Complete your outfit with caps, beanies, crossbody bags, and simple jewelry like chains or rings.

    • Abo White beanie (Php.349)
  • Get Inspired: Browse BINI Mikha's Instagram for streetwear ideas and see how you can adapt her style to your own wardrobe.

Both minimalist and streetwear styles are fantastic for fashion, each offering a unique balance. On some days, you may prefer a laid-back yet stylish look, while on others, you might opt for something simple and elegant. Just as your favorite artist, BINI Mikha, seamlessly blends high fashion with street style, Abo provides a neutral counterpart to bright designer pieces, delivering timeless and balanced style.
